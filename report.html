Report Draft

Information about how I currently plan the report to look like

Draft: Outline

  1. Current Situation
    2. Writing about the numbers and facts of voting in Germany, Koblenz:
    - "Wahlbeteiligung"
    - How people vote, report from Votes from 2019
    - How votes have changed
    - Rezos CDU Video, reached millions of voters, might have affected the reduction of votes in CDU
    - How talking with others, many were confused whom to vote for
    - Political Parties became very diverse, the Opinions change often, there are different Kandidates representing different interests and different points of view
  2. The Problem
    3. - Hypothesis: Can applicable Information Management change(improve) the Results of Votes?
    - Improve in the sence make voters more satisfied with their vote?
    - Information for Voting is hard to gather online, especially for small kommunal votes
    - People barely have time to inform themselves in the busy days
    - there is an online tool called "Wahl-o-mat", used quite often, but it only showes the resulting party
  3. Sulution idea of this paper
    4. A tool that shows all the candidates from one area as a List
    The Candidates can be filtered by specific properties
    e. g.:
    - the party
    - the age
    - the gender
    - the academic title
    - the admisistrative area
    - the interests as hashtags
  4. The system
    5. A Databank that stores all the Candidates Information, being
    - the party
    - the number on the list in the party
    - the title
    - the gender
    - the first name
    - the last name
    - the age
    - the picture as jpeg or png (link)
    - a free text (profession, political engagement)
    - hashtags generated from the free text
    - the administrative area

    Note: Use Delfmanns Unification using Vectors and unify them with a Thesaurus and TF/IDF
    in order to to get Hashtags and unify them
  5. The survey
    6. Find out using the survey, if: - What people thing about the voting process in general
    - If people would appreciate and profit from voting information management
    - If people like a tool like this and if it has the potential to change the voting results

    Ask a significant amount of people, pilot maybe 5 people
    1. Part: find out how voting was for the participants this year
    e. g.
    - did you vote? (yes, no)
    - If yes, how did you vote? (booth, "Briefwahl")
    - How did you vote? (specific Candidates, the whole party, according to the academin title, none of the above)
    - How much time did you invest in the voting process (inform yourself upfront, in the booth)
    - How do you like the voting process / decision making process? (easy, frustrating)

    2. Part: Let participants choose some Kandidates or ask them to choose 56 Kandidates
    - guide them through the tool
    - let them use the tool
    - let them print out the result if the want, on send it per email

    3. Part: Get the participants impression
    - If paricipants did not go voting, would they change their mind with this?
    - How was the process with this tool? Still long/frustrating??
    - Likert Skale: how different is it approx. from your original voting from this year?
    - Would you use a tool like this for your next vote?/ How helpful was it for you(likert scale)
    - Demographics of the Participant
  6. Survey Result & Analysis
    7. - display the results of these questions - Significance tests?
  7. Discussing the survey Results
    8. - Point our what was different from the Assumption
    - Was there a significant results that supports the fact that it changing the voting results? - Was there a significant amout of participants who find the tool helpful / would use it again?
  8. Possible Future Aspects
    9. - Parties import their candidate information (evtl. mass Import with .CSV?) -> All the information gathered in one place
    - Querry on website: Postalcode, directs the voters directly to the right candidates
    - Prevoting analysis with anonymously collected data
    - Smartphone app: helps you vote even in the booth?
    - Apply Cryptography to make it more reliable
  9. Limitations
    10. - security
    - maintenance
    - would still lead to no desired results
  10. Conclusion
    11. I will make my conclustion at the end of the project
